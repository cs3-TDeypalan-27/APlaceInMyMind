A Place In My Mind

BY: Therese Deypalan

Today is one of those sad days

So I went to one familiar place

A safe place to show my fears

As my eyes slowly fill up with tears

I finally felt the comfort that I really needed right here

Then that voice approached me and said

"Its time to leave this paradise"

The clouds slowly clattered my head

Reality came to me and I realized

I was just back in my room again
